One week in Panama was not enough to enjoy all the wonderful landscapes this country has to offer. We felt like a kid in an free ice cream factory! Our trip to Panama was quite spontaneous, like almost all nice trips we have done! Our plan was flying from Germany to Colombia as cheap as possible without stopping in USA (visa issues, bla, bla, you know). When we were about to book a trip that was not as cheap as the way we wanted, when we figured out that if we did a short stop in Panama, the cost would decrease reasonably. Many people book direct flights to save time, but we wanted to save money and why not travelling more. We checked the options and found out that the price was the same for a 12hours stop as it was for one week stop, so we book our adventure including Panama for the same price! Despite Panama City has many things to offer to nature lovers like us, we didn't spend much time in Panama city. For sure next time! I had seen beautiful pics from Bocas del Toro so we planned most of our stay there. Our expectations were quite high for the short time but Panama didn't disappoint us. We flew from Germany to Panama City, then from Panama City to David. There, we took small regional bus from David to Almirante. The view was spectacular! I am from a Caribbean country but still it was fascinating to me, see how the jungle and the sea live in an incredible harmony there. The bus lets you in Almirante as this is the place where the lanchas take you to Bocas. As soon as you go out the bus, there are plenty of people asking you to take a ride to the place where the lanchas are. After more than 24 hours travelling we didn't want to do any effort so we decided to pay the 5 dollars ride to the lanchas place. Actually, this is not necessary. it's about 40 min 3,3 km on foot. The lanchas are about 20 US dollars per person (If you are from Panama you get a discount.) There are different groups depending on the island you are going. We stayed three days in Isla Solarte and three days in Bocas. For the short stay, these islands offered us a tasteful tropical experience!
